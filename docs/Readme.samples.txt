sample_0.txt is the output when simply grinding for a Whirring Contraption.
sample_1.txt is the same, but with detailed information on all actions which have to be performed.
sample_2.txt is like 0, but with echo grinding as a background activity.
sample_3.txt is the same, but with detailed infos again.

sample_4.txt lists the most efficient Echo grinds. sample_5.txt is the same, with --favours enabled, and 6 is the same with all cards made available.
7-9 are the same for scrip.

sample10.txt is an example of calculating (almost) all item grinds (with Echo grinding in the background).
